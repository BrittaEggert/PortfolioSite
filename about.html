About

My story

Originally I am from Germany, but I love to explore the world.
I've lived in Egypt, in Sweden, and settled just currently in Cyprus.
My favorite hobby is horses. I love to spend time at the stable and
work with and ride horses. When I am not at the stables I love to
explore the island, visit the beaches, and ride mountain bike together
with my husband and our kids. In afternoons we like to play board games
and on Fridays evenings we regularly come together to spend time eating popcorn,
cuddling with our dog Fussel and watching movies.

In 2004 I started my career as self-employed but I have as well worked as
a contractor and as a team member in marketing agencies. I could acquire a strong
foundation and understanding in areas such as logo-, print- and web design,
and all areas of UX Design. I am known as quality-oriented, easy to work with and organized.
